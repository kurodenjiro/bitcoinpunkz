How To Buy $BitcoinPunkZ

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet
 Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list.

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask
Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a «0x»

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $BitcoinPunkZ
Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address to select BitcoinPunkZ. Set the slippage tolerance to 19% (sometimes it may be 23-25%, depending on how much demand there is)

View $BitcoinPunkZ and HODL
Swap BNB for BitcoinPunkZ. Now you need to add BitcoinPunkZ to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet to view your $BitcoinPunkZ. Lastly HODL!

Will my rewards always be paid hourly?

Our reward system is triggered by volume (buy + sell activity to cover gas fees). Payouts to holders may not occur every hour if trading volume is low. When this is the
case, the amount of rewarded Bitcoin each holder will receive does not decrease. However, will accrue over a longer period of time until it is suitable for a payout to trigger
(usually a few hours).
